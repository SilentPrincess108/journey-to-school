# ESCAPING BRAINROT

### Introduction
This is a platformer game that chronicles the average student experience. Your task is to arrive at school energetically in order to strive for good grades, while avoiding various distractions, such as those brain rot and memes your friends use to annoy you,  maintaining high energy levels by eating as much nourishing food as you can! Be careful, as each can affect your grades!! 

### Instruction
W, up key, and space to jump; a and left key to move left; d and right key to move right.
